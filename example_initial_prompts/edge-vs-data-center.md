# Example Initial Prompt: Edge vs. Data Center AI

This is an intentionally rough request. It is input to the Prompt Architect,
not an approved charter.

I want to understand what is going to win over the next 20 years: AI running
in centralized data centers, on the edge, or some hybrid of the two. I do not
want to begin with stock picking. I want fundamental research first.

Look at where AI and model research is going. Are models getting larger, or
are they being quantized, distilled, and made cheaper and faster? Are models
moving toward cloud deployment or local/on-device deployment? What happens
with humanoid robots, autonomous systems, phones, PCs, enterprise software,
and real-time applications?

I want the research to look at papers and technical work from leading AI
labs, as well as model and inference data. Look at OpenRouter, Artificial
Analysis, and other useful model measurement sources. Also look at expert
commentary on X/Twitter, including people with substantial technical
experience from major research labs or universities. Find high-signal people,
not random viral commentary.

I want multiple independent research agents with different perspectives.
Each agent should find its own sources, state what it thinks, and then review
the other agents' work honestly. A supervisor should examine the evidence,
the grades, the disagreements, and the strongest counterarguments before
deciding which explanations are best supported.

After the technical research is complete, I eventually want to understand
which structural layers and companies could benefit. That investment work
must come after the technical thesis and should include bear cases and
disconfirming evidence.
